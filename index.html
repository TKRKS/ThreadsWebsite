Threads Overview

Modern computers are used in every part of our daily lives. Because of this, the field of Computer Science has become extremely broad with many different applications. Rather than trying to teach you a little bit of every little part of Computer Science, Georgia Techâ€™s College of Computing has divided the field into eight specializations known as Threads. Each of these Threads focus on a specific part of Computer Science and teaches students the information for that specific specialization. Georgia Tech Computer Science majors must take the classes for two different Threads, giving them a much more focused knowledge of Computer Science that will help them in the specific jobs they find interesting.

The eight Threads are Devices, Information Internetworks, Intelligence, Media, Modeling and Simulation, People, Systems and Architecture and Theory. Click on a Thread below to get more information about that specific Thread.

Intellegence

Computers are getting smarter every day. Whether its being able to find groups of data within huge datasets or simply playing chess, computers are being used more and more often to do tasks that require thinking smart. This is what the Intellegence thread is all about- making computers think and act rationally in situations that typically would require a human. In the Intellegence thread, you will learn how to make a computer act rationally in many different situations. If you like psychology or robotics, this thread may be just for you.

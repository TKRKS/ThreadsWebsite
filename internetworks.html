Information Internetworks

The Internet has become a major part of our everyday lives. More and more electronic devices are being hooked up to the Internet and given networking capabilities. In fact, many industries exist today that would not even be possible without the Internet. There are a lot of individual pieces that combine together that make the Internet possible. These pieces of the Internet make the basis of the Information Internetworks thread.

The Information Internetworks thread deals with the Internet and many different topics that explain how it works. For example, you could learn computer networking, which explains how data is transmitted between different computers. Perhaps you are interested in information security and how to transport sensitive information between computers safely. All of these topic and more can be found in the Information Internetworks thread.

People of many interests will find things to like about the Information Internetworks thread. Obviously, fans of the Internet will enjoy learning how it works and how to make successful programs that run on multiple computers. In addition, fans of low level programming will enjoy this thread. While there is not quite as much low level programming as the Devices or Systems and Architecture threads, there is still a lot that relies on computer hardware, especially with the computer networking and information security classes. In fact, the primary language of this thread is C, just like the other low level threads.

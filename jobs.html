Computer Science Jobs

Algorithm Researcher

Algorithms Researchers are the people who come up with all the clever tricks others use to make a program complete a specific task quickly and efficiently. They develop, test and prove their methods will finish in a short time and using little space if necessary. Algorithm Researchers will focus on the Theory Thread. The Theory Thread will teach you the math necessary not only to develop new algorithms, but also the math needed to prove they will work and work well.

Related Threads:

  • Theory

Compiler Writer

Without programming languages, it would be so incredibly difficult to program for computers that they likely wouldn’t be in widespread use today. Compiler Writers are the people that translate from the high level programming languages that allow people easily create programs to the low level instructions that the computer can actually understand. Compiler Writers need the Systems and Architecture and Theory Thread to do this. The Systems and Architecture Thread will help you understand both computer hardware and how to tell the computer directly to do tasks using assembly language instructions. The Theory Thread is necessary to be able to figure out how actually translate from the high level programming languges to the low level instructions using state machines.

Related Threads:

  • Systems and Architecture
  • Theory

Computer Animator

Computer Animators use computers to create animations that people watch for their entertainment. From children’s cartoons to CGI used in movies to cutscenes in video games, Computer Animators can create these spectacular animations. The Media and People Threads are the most useful for a Computer Animator. The Media Thread will teach you how to use computer graphics and how to actually animate things on the computer screen. The People Thread will teach you how to create visuals that are appealing so people will enjoy watching your animations even more.

Related Threads:

  • Media
  • People

Computer Architect

Computer Architecture is the process of designing both computer hardware and the instructions used to make the hardware do work. A Computer Architect is the person who actually plans out how hardware will integrate with a computer and the instructions that can be used to run them. Since a Computer Architect works so closely with computer hardware, they will find the Devices and Systems and Architecture Threads most useful. The Devices Thread has many computer engineering classes that will teach you about how computer hardware works and is built, which will be needed to make design decisions. The Systems and Architecture Thread will teach you how this hardware will interact with the software being run on it, so that a Computer Architect can make design decisions that will allow the computer to run as smoothly as possible.

Related Threads:

  • Devices
  • Systems and Architecture

Database Administrator

Database Administrators set up and run databases for various companies and agencies. They spend their time designing and implementing the logical database layout (so other programmers can easily access the data) as well as managing the hardware to ensure the database runs as quickly and efficiently as possible. This job lends itself to two Threads- Information Internetworks and Systems and Architecture. In Information Internetworks, there are multiple classes that focus on the logical database layout as well as a few that look at the hardware of the database. As database efficiency deals directly with the operating systems and the underlying computer architecture, the Systems and Architecture Thread will give you the background needed keep your database running efficiently.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • Systems and Architecture

Data Miner

Data Miners take extremely large amounts of data and try to find trends and hidden relations that will help their company make decisions in the future. Data Miners write programs which can sift through terabytes of completely unrelated data and find specific pieces of data that can be grouped together or categorized. The most useful Threads for Data Miners are Information Internetworks and Intelligence. The Information Internetworks Thread will teach you how to deal with the huge amounts of data most modern companies have. The Intelligence Thread will teach you techniques to find useful information among all of this data.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • Intelligence

Data Warehousing Specialist

Data Warehousing Specialists take the enormous databases of large companies and try to combine them in some way to get useful information. They need to be able to work with many different kinds of databases and know how to group and visualize the data to make it as usable as possible. As such, Data Warehousing Specialists will find Information Internetworks and People the most useful Threads. The Information Internetworks Thread will give you the background in databases you need to work with the systems on such a large scale. The People Thread will teach you how to visualize the data in such a way that it will be extremely easy for other people in your company to use.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • People

Graphic Designer

Graphic Designers use computers to create visuals that are used in all kinds of places. They use computers to create memorable designs for websites, brochures and more that will help their companies be more memorable than the competition. Graphic Designers will find Media and People to be the most useful Threads. The Media Thread will teach you how to use computers to create the impressive visuals that make the most memorable graphics. The People Thread will teach you how to create designs that are visually appealing and incredibly helpful to the end user.

Related Threads:

  • Media
  • People

High Frequency Trader

High Frequency Traders are people who use computers to make trades on the stock market so fast that even if the price of a stock has increased by a fraction of a penny, it is enough to make thousands of dollars in a fraction of a second. These people need to write programs that can predict the stock market and quickly react to that predictions. The Modeling and Simulation Thread is ideal for this job. In Modeling and Simulation, you will learn how to create and program models which can be used to help predict changes in the stock market. In addition, you will learn how to run these models on incredibly fast computers so that you can make these trades fast enough to earn a profit.

Related Threads:

  • Modeling and Simulation

Mobile Developer

Mobile Developers create programs that are run on phones and other small devices. Whether they are making a simple calendar system or apps for popular websites, Mobile Developers must always focus on both the limited hardware they have to work with and the fact that the phones are always on the move. Mobile Developers will find the Devices and the Information Internetworks Threads most useful. The Devices Thread teaches you how to write programs that are designed specifically to run on limited hardware. The Information Internetworks Thread will teach you how to stay connected to a mobile device that is always on the move (which is much harder than it sounds).

Related Threads:

  • Devices
  • Information Internetworks

Modeling Engineer

Modeling Engineers develop and programs models that can simulate all kinds of events. From the weather prediction to the latest scientific theories, Modeling Engineers are there to test them out and use them for extrapolation. The Threads most useful for Modeling Engineers are Modeling and Simulation and Theory. The Modeling and Simulation Thread will teach you how to develop, program and run these simulations as quickly as possible. The Theory Thread will teach you the math needed to help understand these models well enough to correctly implement them.

Related Threads:

  • Modeling and Simulation
  • Theory

Network Administrator

Network Administrators set up and run large scale networks for different companies. They need to make the network run quickly, run efficiently and be resilient against all kinds of failure. The Information Internetworks and Devices Threads will be extremely helpful to a Network Administrator. The Information Internetworks Thread will teach you the networking you need in order understand how to set up and run these networks in addition to teaching you security to help protect them. In addition, the Devices Thread will help teach you how the hardware that connects to the networks actually works, so you can know what to expect when you add additional nodes to your network.

Related Threads:

  • Devices
  • Information Internetworks

Operating Systems Designer

Operating Systems are some of the largest and most complicated programs in existence. As such, working on them is a very specialized job. Operating Systems Designers develop and add on to operating systems that take care of the computer hardware so other programs don’t have to worry about it. The Systems and Architecture Thread is perfect for an Operating Systems Designer. This entire Thread is focused on computer hardware and its interaction with computer software (in fact, Operating System Design is one of the main classes for the Thread), so it will be a great help for an Operating System Designer.

Related Threads:

  • Systems and Architecture

Roboticist

Roboticists design, build and program robots that do all kinds of different tasks. Whether they are working on simple robots that work on assembly lines or complex robots that play soccer, Roboticists must be familiar with the computer hardware necessary to build the robots as well as the algorithms needed for the robot to run on its own. The two most useful Threads for a Roboticist would be Devices and Intelligence. The Devices Thread will teach you about the computer hardware and how to write programs that work with very limited hardware (which is useful because robots may not be able to fit a full scale computer within them). The Intelligence Thread will teach you about algorithms which will allow the robot to work autonomously without input from the Roboticist.

Related Threads:

  • Devices
  • Intelligence

Search Specialist

Search Specialists create and maintain the search engines that are used to find new and useful websites on the Internet. They must be able to create the programs that will find new websites and find ways to figure out what website best match the incoming queries. The Information Internetworks, Intelligence and Theory Threads are all useful for a Search Specialist. The Information Internetworks Thread will teach you how to sift through all the different websites and find the most useful ones for a specific query. The Intelligence Thread will teach you how to write the programs that are needed to find the websites in the first place (since the Internet is too big to manually search). The Theory Thread will teach you the algorithms needed to both find the best websites and to return the most accurate results possible.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • Intelligence
  • Theory

Security Specialist

Security Specialists are the the defenders of the computer world. They work to stop hackers, viruses and more from hurting people and their computers. Since hackers target a wide variety of computers and programs, Security Specialists must understand both how the internals of computers and the Internet works. As such, the best Threads for this job would be Information Internetworks and Systems and Architecture. In Information Internetworks, you will learn both the basics of information security that can protect information both on and between computers, and how the Internet works so you are aware how viruses can transfer themselves. In addition, since virus often target bugs deep in the operating system code, the Systems and Architecture Thread will give you a good basis necessary to combat these attacks.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • Systems and Architecture

Video Game Designer

The video game industry has become a multibillion dollar industry in the past decade. As such, they always need new people who can program these giant, complex games. Video Game Designers program these video games, often with their own specific specialization. Depending on your specific interests, a student who wants to be a Video Game Designer may find any of the Information Internetworks, Intelligence, Media, and People Threads useful. The Information Internetworks Thread will teach a student how to make two computers interact with each other, which is needed if you want to make an online multiplayer game. The Intelligence Thread is great for learning how to program artificial intelligence for the non-player characters in your game. The Media Thread will teach you how to program computer graphics to make your game look nice. Finally, the People Thread will teach you how to make easy to use user interfaces that will allow anyone to easily pick up and play your game. Depending on your specific end goal as a Video Game Designer, any of these Threads would give you foundation needed to make your game.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • Intelligence
  • Media
  • People

Web Designer

Web Designers create websites that people use every day. They have to both make a front end that is appealing and intuitive to users and create the back end interactions that will allow the site to run well on the Internet. As such, Web Designers would find the Information Internetworks and People Threads most useful. The Information Internetworks Thread will give you the knowledge of the Internet necessary to create a back end to the website that will work with all the users who will view it online. The People Thread will teach you how to create intuitive and helpful user interfaces that will grab the user’s attention and keep them coming back for more.

Related Threads:

  • Information Internetworks
  • People
